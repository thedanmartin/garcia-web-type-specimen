Capitalics Warsaw type foundry is team of font designers who are located in Warsaw, Poland. MICHAŁ JAROCINSKI is the founder of the Capitalics foundry who created the organization in 2007 and has created many fonts including Tzimmes, Clavo, Servus and of course - macho. the Capitalics website desribed macho as “Font Macho, designed by Michał Jarocinski, has a complex nature. He’s a true “gentle giant”. He kicks asses as a splendid fighter, but also, being a gentle lover, leaves girls breathless.”
